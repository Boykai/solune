# Settings

The Settings page lets you configure Solune's behavior, including AI model preferences and Signal notification integration.

## What You See

### Primary Settings

These are the settings you are most likely to adjust:

- **AI Configuration**
  - **Model** — choose which AI model your agents use (e.g., GPT-4, GPT-3.5)
  - **Temperature** — adjust the creativity/randomness of AI responses (lower = more focused, higher = more creative)
  - **System prompt** — customize the base instructions given to agents

- **Signal Integration**
  - **Connection status** — shows whether your Signal account is linked
  - **Connect / Disconnect** — link or unlink a Signal account for receiving notifications
  - **QR code** — when connecting, scan the QR code with your Signal app to complete the link

## How to Use It

### Changing AI Settings

1. Open the **AI Configuration** section
2. Select your preferred model from the dropdown
3. Adjust the temperature slider to your liking
4. Optionally edit the system prompt to customize agent behavior
5. Save your changes

### Connecting Signal

1. In the **Signal Integration** section, click **Connect**
2. A QR code appears on screen
3. Open Signal on your phone → Settings → Linked Devices → Link New Device
4. Scan the QR code
5. Once linked, the status updates to "Connected"

### Saving Changes

After editing any settings, make sure to save before leaving the page. If you have unsaved changes and try to close the browser or reload, Solune will warn you.

## Tips

- Most users only need to set their AI model and optionally connect Signal.
- User settings load when you open the page — you may see a brief loading indicator.
